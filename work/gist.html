A professional yet playful dynamic brand identity.

A team branding project called ‘Gist’, collaborated with Dylan Maguire, Sophie Dodds-Mah, Melissa Clissold and myself. The word ‘Gist’ is a name that identifies and unifies our team. This project involved a creation of a dynamic brand identity that is not only a recognisable and stable system, but is also fluid and adaptive. The dynamic identity has many colour and pattern variations that can be applied to communication materials.
