A hybrid display typeface that reflects my personal identity.

Manchuria is a hybrid display typeface that carries distinct characteristics inspired by Chinese and Russian typography. This typeface does not only reflect a personal identity, it also appeal and raise awareness of the history and culture in the Manchuria region, whilst maintaining authenticity for westerners through typography in the English language.
